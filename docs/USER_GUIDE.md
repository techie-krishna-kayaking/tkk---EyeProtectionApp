# User Guide

## Installing

Download the build for your platform from the
[Releases](../../releases) page (see the table in the README) and run it. On
first launch, grant **notification permission** when prompted.

## Daily use

EyeGuard lives quietly in your **system tray** (Windows) or **menu bar**
(macOS), and runs in the background on mobile. Every hour (configurable) it
shows a gentle reminder:

> 👀 **Eye Exercise Time** — Take just 30 seconds.

Choose one of:

- **Done** — opens the 30-second guided routine and records a completion.
- **Snooze 5 min** — reminds you again shortly.
- **Skip** — dismisses this reminder.

### The guided routine

Follow the on-screen animated eye through five steps — look away, blink, roll
clockwise, roll anti-clockwise, and breathe — then enjoy the completion
celebration. 🎉

## In a meeting?

When your microphone is active, EyeGuard stays silent. A few minutes after your
call ends, it quietly resurfaces the reminder you missed.

## Dashboard

See today's reminders, completions, your current streak, and weekly / monthly
trends.

## Settings

- **Reminder interval** — 30 / 45 / 60 / 90 / 120 minutes.
- **Pause during meetings** — on/off.
- **Notification sound** — on/off.
- **Start at login** — launch automatically when you sign in (desktop).
- **Theme** — System / Light / Dark.
- **Reset / Export** — manage your data.

## Tray / menu-bar menu

Right-click the icon for quick actions: Open, Remind me now, Snooze, Settings,
Quit.
